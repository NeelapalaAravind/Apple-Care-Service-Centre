About Us

The Apple Store is a chain of retail stores owned and operated by Apple Inc. These stores offer a wide range of Apple products

  • iPhones
  • iPads
  • MacBooks
  • Apple Watches
  • Accessories

    • They also provide various services such as:

     
Technical support at the Genius Bar
Personal setup for new devices
Educational sessions through the “Today at Apple” program1.
The first Apple Stores were opened in May 2001 by Steve Jobs, with locations in Tysons, Virginia, and Glendale, California1. Since then, Apple has expanded to over 500 stores in 27 countries1. The stores are known for their sleek design, open spaces, and customer-friendly environment.

    Genuine

    All Apple Stores feature a Genius Bar, where customers can receive technical advice or set up service and repair for their products. The Genius Bar provides hardware service on products that are not classified vintage or obsolete.[98] However, in most cases the Geniuses will at least attempt to assist customers with older hardware
